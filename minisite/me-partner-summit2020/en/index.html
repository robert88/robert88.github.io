Huawei Middle East Ecosystem Partner Summit 2019

20th February,2019| Waldorf Astoria The Palm, Dubai

Huawei cordially invites you to attend Middle East Enterprise Ecosystem Partner Summit 2019, which will be held in Dubai, United Arab Emirates on February 20th, 2019. The event is estimated to host more than 200 delegates from our Regional Distributors, Named Channel, Global & Technology partners. Attending from over 12 countries, will gather to exchange the success stories and explore new business opportunities in a Win-win Ecosystem.

Huawei along with the sponsors will jointly host the Ecosystem Partner Summit under the theme of "Leading Connectivity & Intelligence". We invite you to experience and be part of our strength in Artificial Intelligence through our AI Enabled Products & Solutions portfolio.

We appreciate your continued support for Huawei and look forward to meeting you.

Huawei is working with leading global customers and partners to practice joint innovation and digital transformation. Learn how Huawei has partnered with Global Fortune 100 and 500 companies to transform a broad range of industries.
