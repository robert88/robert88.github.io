March 8, 2020 | Dubai, UAE

Invitation

Dear Valued Partner,

On behalf of Huawei, I cordially invite you to attend Huawei Eco-Connect Middle East 2020, which will be held at the Waldorf Astoria, The Palm, Dubai on March 8th, 2020.

Huawei, along with our sponsors, will jointly host the Huawei Eco-Connect Middle East 2020 under the theme of “Win Together". The event is estimated to host more than 300 delegates from our regional distributors, named channel, global partners & technology and alliance partners. Partners from across the Middle East region will gather to exchange success stories and explore new business opportunities in a Win-Win Ecosystem. Together, we aim to seize the opportunities that the digital and intelligent era brings.

At the event, we will demonstrate how the development of Wi-Fi 6, 5G, and AI technologies is enabling significant advancement in the application, network, and coverage of enterprise campuses. In addition, you will see how the new-generation data center is becoming faster, more stable, and more energy efficient with leading AI-powered All-Flash storage.

We will also introduce new intelligent campus solutions “HiCampus” and intelligent datacenter solutions “HiDC” as part of our Horizon Digital Platform. You will gain insights into how the Horizon Digital Platform integrates new ICT including AI, machine learning and IoT, to help enterprises reduce complexity and build an intelligent future, where everything connects.

Together, let’s build a fully connected, intelligent world. We will share the full day conference & gala dinner agenda with you in the coming days.

I look forward to welcoming you in Dubai.

Terry He

President, Enterprise Business Group, Middle East Region, Huawei
