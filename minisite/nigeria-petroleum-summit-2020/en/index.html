Nigeria International Petroleum Summit

February 10-12, 2020, ICC, Abuja, Nigeria

Register Get Pricing/Info

The Nigeria International Petroleum Summit (NIPS) 2020 will be held at the International Conference Center (ICC) in Abuja, Nigeria, over February 10–12, 2020. Huawei invites you to visit our stand — B27 — where we will showcase our leading ICT solution for the oil and gas industry, as well as join with MTN Group to launch the 4G eLTE Digital Oil Field solution.

The key highlights of the NIPS 2020 include:

. The official launch of the 4G eLTE Digital Oil Field solution with MTN.
. A technology session, including keynote speeches, on February 10, 2020.
. A workshop themed “Leading New ICT, Enabling Digital Oil & Gas” from 2 p.m. to 4 p.m. on February 11, 2020.
