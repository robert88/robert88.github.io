Welcome to the intelligent era! In 2020, a highly connected, intelligent world will no longer be an abstract idea, it will be a reality, transforming industries and improving health and human lives. The question will be, how do we connect to this new world?

Huawei will arrange specific activities at our exhibition booth at Mobile World Congress 2020 to help governments and enterprises address this question. We will showcase applications and case studies demonstrating the power of digital transformation in global smart cities, transportation, finance, electric power, and other industries.

We will also introduce new intelligent campus solutions “HiCampus” and intelligent data center solutions “HiDC” as part of our Horizon Digital Platform. You will gain insights into how the Horizon Digital Platform integrates new ICT including AI, machine learning and IoT, to help enterprises reduce complexity and build an intelligent future, where everything connects.

We will also demonstrate how the development of Wi-Fi 6, 5G, and AI technologies is enabling significant advancement in the application, network, and coverage of enterprise campuses. In addition, you will see how the new-generation data center is becoming faster, more stable, and more energy efficient with AI technologies.

During the exhibition, Huawei will show how innovative ICT will meet customer requirements for digital transformation and help achieve business success while facilitating the development of an intelligent future.

We will also hold digital transformation summits, technical forums, CXO roundtables, and themed parties to provide our guests with an open platform for discussion.

Standing at the forefront of the intelligent era, Huawei collaborates with ecosystem partners to build the core of this digital world, enabling customers to leverage digital technologies to transform their businesses and lead global technological innovation in their own fields, while helping more customers enter the intelligent era.

We look forward to seeing you at MWC2020. Let's build a better connected, intelligent world together.

Yan Lida
President of Enterprise BG
Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd.
