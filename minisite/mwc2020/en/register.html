  • Time

    Rules
    During the activity period, customers registered on the Huawei Enterprise official website and designated platforms are offered the chance to communicate with Huawei experts at Huawei enterprise MWC 2020 booth.     d. One registered account can apply for one visitor only.
    Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd. reserves the right of final interpretation of expert communications and the right to disqualify any individual who tampers with the registration or entry process.
